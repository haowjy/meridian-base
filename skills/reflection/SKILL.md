# Reflection

Before reporting, step back and review your own work.

1. **Did you complete the task?** Check your output against what was asked.
   What's missing? What did you add that wasn't asked for?
2. **Did you complete it well?** Review as if evaluating someone else's
   work. Verify with whatever tools are available.
3. **Did you follow your core principles?** Re-read the principles loaded
   into your context. Where did you drift?
4. **Fix** what you found. Don't just list problems.
5. **Report** — what you delivered, what you're unsure about, and any
   concerns you couldn't resolve yourself.
