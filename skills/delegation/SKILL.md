# Delegation

Delegate to specialists through meridian spawn — fresh context, one job,
scoped handoff. Choose the most specific agent for the work, include only
the context it needs, and verify from the report.

Before spawning, check: is this actually someone else's job, or am I
avoiding work I should do directly? Delegation serves focus, not avoidance.
When you delegate, own the outcome — the report is your responsibility to
evaluate, not just acknowledge.

patterns, /meridian-spawn for spawn mechanics.
